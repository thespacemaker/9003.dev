Spatial Computing

Spatial computing allows the opportunity to build digital assets into the real world and display them with new and existing technology. With this model, we have revolutionized the music industry, created amazing multiplayer experiences, and currently work alongside businesses to bring their content to life.

9003 Industries, Inc. is pioneering spatial computing on both the B2B and B2C level, offering deployment cross-platform including: iOS/Android, Magic Leap, NReal and more.

Multiplayer

With modern mobile platforms, we can build experiences that can be supported across multiple devices. Users can share experiences with friends and family or over the internet. The power of this artistic license allows a new world of opportunity for the artist to captivate an audience in new and profound ways. We have only begun to be explore this new world.

The replayability of our content is unique to the user. Spatial computing allows the user to view a scene from any angle at any time. Fast forward, rewind, and pause all at your fingertips.

4D Capture

Volumetric Video represents the next evolution in photography/cinematography. By capturing an entire scene perspective rather than the centuries old single vantage point, the assets can be reconstructed and viewed from any point in space.

A new type of asset


No longer restricted to the pages of science fiction, the concept of holograms will change the way we consume and distribute media. Viewable anywhere as opposed to the confinement of traditional 2D content. From entertainment to telepresence, we allow mobile and headset users to view and project Volumetric Video across all digital platforms.
